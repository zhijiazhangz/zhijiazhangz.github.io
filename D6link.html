D6-link from the Burkhardt quartic X4 to a smooth quadric


The page displays in equations the D6-link from the Burkhardt quartic X4 to a smooth quadric introduced in the paper.

		y14 - y1*y23 - y1*y33 - y1*y43 + 3*y2*y3*y4*y5 - y1*y53.
