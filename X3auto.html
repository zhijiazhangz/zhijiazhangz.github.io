X3Automorphism

Subgroups of the automorphism group of X3


The page displays a subgroup lattice of S6 as the automorphism group of the Segre cubic defined in P4 by 
-y[1]^2*y[2] - y[1]*y[2]^2 - y[1]^2*y[3] - 2*y[1]*y[2]*y[3] - y[2]^2*y[3] - y[1]*y[3]^2 - y[2]*y[3]^2 - y[1]^2*y[4] - 2*y[1]*y[2]*y[4] - y[2]^2*y[4] - 2*y[1]*y[3]*y[4] - 2*y[2]*y[3]*y[4] - y[3]^2*y[4] - y[1]*y[4]^2 - y[2]*y[4]^2 - y[3]*y[4]^2 - y[1]^2*y[5] - 2*y[1]*y[2]*y[5] - y[2]^2*y[5] - 2*y[1]*y[3]*y[5] - 2*y[2]*y[3]*y[5] - y[3]^2*y[5] - 2*y[1]*y[4]*y[5] - 2*y[2]*y[4]*y[5] - 2*y[3]*y[4]*y[5] - y[4]^2*y[5] - y[1]*y[5]^2 - y[2]*y[5]^2 - y[3]*y[5]^2 - y[4]*y[5]^2
and the classes of the subgroups which
  • do not fixed any singular points
  • are not subgroups of the nonstandard S5
  • are not S6, A6 and standard S5

The source code of matrices of the groups can be found here.
