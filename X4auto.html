Subgroups of the automorphism group of X4


The page displays a subgroup lattice of PSp4(F3), the automorphism group of the Burkhardt quardtic defined in P4 by 
    y[1]^4-y[1]*(y[2]^3+y[3]^3+y[4]^3+y[5]^3)+3*y[2]*y[3]*y[4]*y[5],
and the classes of the subgroups which
  • do not have cohomology obstructions to linearizability and,
  • do not contain the nonlinearizable C2-involution fixing a K3 surface
The source code of matrices of the groups can be found here.
